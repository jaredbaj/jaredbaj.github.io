momma's boy identity and press campaign

Our band needed a way to shop our new EP to press outlets, promoters, and labels - and have it get noticed. This launch netted a Friday night set in Austin during SXSW.

I oversaw our art direction, wrote major copy, planned our promotional calendar, and managed the new site project from build to launch. Now, I manage analytics (we've increased our subscriber base by 80% since I joined!), handle our digital promotional presence, write/design press and social media materials, and openly encourage the band to sport mustaches.

2016 s/s menswear report

One time, I went to New York and tried to predict the future. The resulting trend report aimed to brief my colleagues on the threads (pun intended) and themes that would continue to gain momentum in men's fashion design and general pop culture moving into 2016. Disclaimer: These trends may not yet have reached the midwest.

My research consisted of reading thousands of words on developments in everything from tech to agriculture, taking street scene photography around Mahattan and Brooklyn, and poring over every runway image I could get my greedy little paws on. The end result served as educational and inspirational material for designers and end consumer marketing pros in the fashion industry.

You can download the whole thing here. Let me know if I missed anything.
